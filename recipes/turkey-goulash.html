Turkey Goulash

Description

Experience a deightful twist on a classic with our Turkey Goulash. Lean ground turkey cooked in a rich, tomato-based sauce with a medley of spices and tender vegetables. It's a flavorful, hearty dish that's as easy to make as it is delicious. Perfect for satisfying your cravings for comfort food with a healthy twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 1 (14 ounch) can stewed, diced tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the turkey until browned.
  2. Stir in the stewed tomatoes, garlic, tomato sauce, sugar and basil, and then simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente, and then drain. Combine the pasta and turkey mixture; toss and serve.
